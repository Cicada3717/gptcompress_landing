Security

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Security is not an afterthought at GPTCompress; it is the foundation of our architecture.

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We designed GPTCompress to be "zero-knowledge" regarding your conversation history whenever possible. We do not persist your conversation logs in any database. The data is processed in ephemeral memory and discarded immediately after the summary is generated.

2. Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using robust Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocols (HTTPS). This ensures that your conversation history cannot be intercepted during transit.

3. Third-Party Processing

We utilize industry-leading AI models (e.g., OpenAI's API) to power our summarization. We transmit data to these providers securely via their official APIs. We ensure our providers adhere to strict data privacy standards (SOC 2, GDPR).

4. Payment Security

We do not store your credit card information. All payments are processed securely by our third-party payment processor, which is PCI-DSS compliant.

5. Vulnerability Reporting

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in GPTCompress, please disclose it to us responsibly by emailing security@gptcompress.com.
