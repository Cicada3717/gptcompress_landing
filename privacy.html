Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

At GPTCompress, we believe your conversation data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Minimization

Our core philosophy is simple: we do not want to hold your data. GPTCompress processes your conversation history in real-time to extract summaries and critical context. Once the processing is complete, the raw data is discarded from our processing memory. We do not store your conversation logs on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous metrics about how features are used to improve the product (e.g., number of summaries generated).
  • Account Information: If you sign up for a paid plan, we collect your email and necessary payment information (processed via secure third-party providers).

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. The conversation text you submit is used exclusively for the purpose of generating the summary you requested.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL protocols.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@gptcompress.com.
